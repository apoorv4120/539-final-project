Yoru

Duelist

Abilities

Fakeout

C

Equip and deploy a Fakeout. The ability creates sound echoes of Yoru moving forward. Activating it again causes the echoes to move in the opposite direction, potentially confusing enemies.

Blindside

Q

Equip and deploy a Blindside. The ability throws a flashbang that briefly blinds enemies in its vicinity.

Gatecrash

E

Equip and deploy a Gatecrash. The ability places a rift tether. Activating it again teleports Yoru to the location of the tether, potentially catching enemies off guard.

Dimensional Drift

X

Equip and activate Dimensional Drift. Yoru enters a dimension rift, becoming invisible and invulnerable to enemies. He can scout the map and reposition himself before returning to his original location.
