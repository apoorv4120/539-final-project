Viper

Controller

Abilities

Poison Cloud

C

Equip and deploy a Poison Cloud emitter. Fire to throw the emitter to the target location. The emitter can be reactivated to create a poisonous smoke cloud at the location for a duration, damaging any players within it.

Toxic Screen

Q

Equip and deploy a long line of gas emitters. Fire to activate a long, poisonous wall in front of Viper. Can be deployed at a long distance, and can be reactivated to create a new wall at a different location.

Snake Bite

E

Equip and throw a throwable cannister. Upon impact, the cannister releases a pool of acid that damages enemies standing within it.

Viper's Pit

X

Equip and cast a chemical container that creates a large cloud of toxic gas at Viper's location. The gas cloud greatly reduces the vision range of all players inside it and damages enemies inside it. Viper can see through the cloud, enabling her to control the area effectively.
