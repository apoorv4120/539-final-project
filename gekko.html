Gekko

Initiator

Abilities

Recon Drones

C

Equip and deploy Recon Drones. The drones fly forward and can be controlled by Gekko to scout ahead, revealing enemy positions in their line of sight.

Stealth Field

Q

Equip and activate a Stealth Field generator. Upon activation, Gekko and nearby allies become invisible to enemy radars and minimaps for a short duration.

EMP Pulse

E

Equip and deploy an EMP Pulse device. The device emits a pulse that disables enemy abilities and electronic devices within its radius for a short duration.

Overdrive

X

Activate Overdrive to enhance Gekko's movement speed and reload speed for a short duration. Additionally, Gekko's attacks deal bonus damage during Overdrive.
