Raze

Duelist

Abilities

Boom Bot

C

Equip and deploy a Boom Bot. The bot moves forward, bouncing off walls and detouring around obstacles. It will lock onto any enemies in its frontal cone and chase them down, exploding on contact and dealing damage.

Blast Pack

Q

Equip and throw a Blast Pack. The pack sticks to surfaces and can be remotely detonated, propelling nearby players and objects into the air.

Paint Shells

E

Equip and throw Paint Shells. The shells explode upon contact with any surface, releasing a cluster of smaller grenades that deal damage to enemies in the area.

Showstopper

X

Equip a rocket launcher. Fire to launch a rocket that travels in a straight line and explodes on contact, dealing heavy damage to enemies caught in the blast radius.
