Killjoy

Sentinel

Abilities

Nano Swarm

C

Equip and throw a grenade. Upon landing, the grenade will release swarms of nanobots that deal damage to enemies in the area. Can be reactivated to detonate early.

Alarmbot

Q

Equip and deploy a bot that hunts down enemies and explodes when in proximity, dealing damage and applying Vulnerable to those caught in the blast radius.

Turret

E

Equip and deploy a turret that automatically fires at enemies within its 180-degree cone. Can be reactivated to move the turret to a new location.

Lockdown

X

Equip and deploy a device that emits a field, which detains all enemies caught in its radius after a brief windup. Enemies caught in the radius are detained, making them unable to move or use abilities for a short duration. The device can be destroyed by enemies.
