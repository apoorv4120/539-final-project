Phoenix

Duelist

Abilities

Curveball

C

Equip and throw a Curveball grenade. The grenade curves around corners and blinds enemies caught in its line of sight.

Hot Hands

Q

Equip and throw a Hot Hands grenade. The grenade detonates upon impact, creating a fire zone that heals Phoenix over time while damaging enemies.

Blaze

E

Equip and deploy a Blaze wall. The wall of flames blocks vision and damages enemies that pass through it. Phoenix can curve the wall to the left or right while deploying it.

Run It Back

X

Activate Run It Back to enter a temporary form. When eliminated during this form, Phoenix is reborn at his location with full health. If the ability duration expires, or if manually canceled, Phoenix returns to his original location with the health he had before activating Run It Back.
