Neon

Duelist

Abilities

Flashbang

C

Equip and throw a Flashbang grenade. The grenade detonates after a short delay, blinding and disorienting enemies caught in its blast radius.

Scanner

Q

Equip and activate a Scanner device. The device emits a pulse that reveals the locations of nearby enemies through walls and obstacles.

Decoy

E

Equip and deploy a Decoy hologram. The hologram mimics Neon's movements and actions, drawing enemy attention away from Neon's actual position.

Phase Shift

X

Activate Phase Shift to enter a phased state, becoming temporarily invulnerable to enemy attacks and able to move through obstacles and enemies. Lasts for a short duration.
