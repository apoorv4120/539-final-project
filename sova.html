Sova

Initiator

Abilities

Shock Bolt

C

Equip a bow with a shock bolt. Fire to send the explosive bolt forward, detonating upon impact and damaging all nearby enemies.

Owl Drone

Q

Equip and deploy an owl drone. Fire to control the movement of the drone. While in control of the drone, Fire to shoot a marking dart. This dart will reveal the location of any player struck by the dart.

Recon Bolt

E

Equip a bow with a recon bolt. Fire to send the recon bolt forward, activating upon impact and revealing the location of nearby enemies caught in the line of sight of the bolt.

Hunter's Fury

X

Equip a bow with three long-range, wall-piercing energy blasts. Fire to release an energy blast in a line in front of Sova, dealing damage and revealing the location of enemies caught in the line.
