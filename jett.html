Jett

Duelist

Abilities

Cloudburst

C

Equip and throw a Cloudburst grenade. The grenade creates a brief vision-obscuring cloud of smoke upon impact with the ground.

Updraft

Q

Equip and jump. Upon activation, Jett propels herself vertically, allowing for quick repositioning or accessing elevated areas.

Tailwind

E

Equip and dash a short distance in the direction she's moving. The ability allows for swift repositioning or aggressive plays.

Blade Storm

X

Equip and unleash a flurry of throwing knives. The knives deal moderate damage and can be thrown individually or all at once. Eliminating an enemy player with Blade Storm resets its duration and refills the player's knives.
