Sage

Sentinel

Abilities

Barrier Orb

C

Equip and deploy a Barrier Orb. The orb forms a large, solid wall upon activation, blocking enemy movement and gunfire.

Slow Orb

Q

Equip and throw a Slow Orb. The orb detonates upon impact with the ground, creating a field that slows down enemy movement and firing rates.

Healing Orb

E

Equip and activate a Healing Orb. The orb emits healing energy, restoring health to Sage or an ally within its radius.

Resurrection

X

Equip and activate Resurrection. Sage channels energy to resurrect a fallen ally back to life with full health.
