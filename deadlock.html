Deadlock

Sentinel

Abilities

Flashbang

C

Equip and throw a Flashbang grenade. The grenade detonates after a short delay, blinding and disorienting enemies caught in its blast radius.

Smoke Canister

Q

Equip and deploy a Smoke Canister. The canister releases a cloud of smoke upon activation, obscuring vision and providing cover for Deadlock and her teammates.

Decoy

E

Equip and deploy a Decoy hologram. The hologram mimics Deadlock's movements and actions, drawing enemy attention away from Deadlock's actual position.

Lockdown

X

Equip and activate Lockdown. Deadlock deploys a lockdown device that disrupts enemy movement and abilities within its radius. After a brief delay, the device detonates, stunning and leaving enemies vulnerable.
