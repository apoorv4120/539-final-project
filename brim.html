Brimstone

Controller

Abilities

Stim Beacon

C

Equip and deploy a Stim Beacon. The beacon emits a healing aura and increases the firing rate of Brimstone and his teammates within its radius.

Incendiary

Q

Equip and throw an Incendiary grenade. The grenade detonates upon impact, creating a fire zone that damages enemies over time and forces them to reposition.

Sky Smoke

E

Equip and deploy a Sky Smoke grenade. The grenade releases a smoke cloud upon activation, obscuring vision and providing cover for Brimstone and his teammates.

Orbital Strike

X

Equip and activate Orbital Strike. Brimstone calls in a devastating orbital strike at a targeted location, dealing massive damage to enemies within its radius.
