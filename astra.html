Astra

Controller

Abilities

Gravity Well

C

Equip and deploy a Gravity Well. The well draws in nearby enemies and disrupts their movement, pulling them towards the center before exploding and briefly leaving affected enemies vulnerable.

Nova Pulse

Q

Equip and throw a Nova Pulse. The pulse charges up before detonating, concussing all players within its radius and briefly leaving them disoriented.

Nebula

E

Equip and deploy a Nebula. The nebula creates a cloud of smokescreen that obscures vision and provides cover for Astra and her teammates.

Astral Form / Cosmic Divide

X

Activate Astral Form to enter the astral plane. From the astral plane, Astra can place stars to use her other abilities. While in Astral Form, she can also activate Cosmic Divide, a large, stationary wall that divides the map and blocks enemy vision and gunfire.
