Chamber

Sentinel

Abilities

Echoes of Chaos

C

Equip and throw a pair of twin daggers. Upon impact, the daggers will create a shadow clone of Chamber, mimicking her movements and actions for a short duration. The shadow clone will attack enemies with Chamber's basic attack, dealing damage.

Shade Step

Q

Activate to teleport a short distance in the direction Chamber is facing. Can be used to quickly reposition during combat or evade enemy attacks.

Shadow Strike

E

Activate to imbue Chamber's next basic attack with shadow energy, causing it to deal bonus damage and apply a slowing effect to the target.

Umbral Assault

X

Activate to enter a shadow form, becoming untargetable and gaining increased movement speed. While in shadow form, Chamber can pass through obstacles and enemies. Lasts for a short duration or until Chamber attacks.
