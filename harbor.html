Harbor

Controller

Abilities

Barrier Orb

C

Equip and deploy a Barrier Orb. The orb creates a large, solid wall that blocks enemy vision and movement.

Aegis Alarm

Q

Equip and deploy an Aegis Alarm. The alarm can be placed on surfaces and will trigger a loud noise when an enemy is nearby, providing information on enemy positions.

Harbor's Haven

E

Equip and deploy Harbor's Haven. The haven creates a protective area that boosts the health regeneration of Harbor and nearby allies.

Guardian Angel

X

Activate Guardian Angel to call upon a powerful guardian entity. The guardian provides enhanced protection to Harbor and nearby allies, increasing their durability and reducing incoming damage.
