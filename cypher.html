Cypher

Sentinel

Abilities

Trapwire

C

Equip and deploy a tripwire. The wire connects to nearby walls, creating a tripwire that activates upon enemy contact, briefly revealing and tethering the enemy's location.

Cyber Cage

Q

Equip and deploy a Cyber Cage. The cage creates a zone that blocks vision and slows enemies who pass through it. It can be activated to create noise and briefly obscure vision, potentially tricking enemies.

Spycam

E

Equip and deploy a Spycam. The cam can be placed on surfaces and activated to remotely view its camera feed. It can also shoot tracking darts to reveal enemy locations.

Neural Theft

X

Equip and use Neural Theft to extract information from a dead enemy player's corpse. This reveals the locations of all living enemy players.
