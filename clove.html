Clove

Controller

Abilities

Smoke Screen

C

Equip and deploy a Smoke Screen. The screen obscures vision and provides cover for Clove and teammates.

Nebula

Q

Equip and deploy a Nebula. The nebula creates a cloud of gas that blocks vision and damages enemies who enter it.

Gravity Well

E

Equip and deploy a Gravity Well. The well creates a vortex that pulls in nearby enemies and prevents them from moving.

Black Hole

X

Equip and activate Black Hole. Clove summons a powerful black hole at a targeted location, pulling in nearby enemies and dealing massive damage.
