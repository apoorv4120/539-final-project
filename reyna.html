Reyna

Duelist

Abilities

Leer

C

Equip an ethereal, destructible eye. Activate to cast the eye a short distance forward. The eye will Nearsight all enemies who look at it.

Devour

Q

Kill an enemy to consume their soul, gaining full health. Devour will automatically cast if Reyna is not at full health when the enemy is killed.

Dismiss

E

Instantly consume a nearby soul orb, becoming intangible for a short duration.

Empress

X

Instantly enter a frenzy, increasing firing speed, equip and reload speed dramatically. Scoring a kill renews the duration.
