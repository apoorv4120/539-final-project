Fade

Controller

Abilities

Fadeout

C

Equip a Fadeout drone. Fire to deploy and control the drone. The drone emits a field that makes players within it unable to take damage and reduces incoming healing by 50%.

Burst

Q

Equip a Burst. Fire to throw a Burst that explodes after a short fuse, dealing damage to enemies caught in its area. The Burst can also be cooked to detonate it earlier.

Phase

E

Equip a Phase. Fire to throw a Phase that attaches to surfaces and emits a field that makes players invisible and reduces their footstep noise.

Eclipse

X

Equip an Eclipse ult. Fire to activate a powerful, short-range burst of energy that makes players invisible, reduces their footstep noise, and disables aim assist for a short duration.
