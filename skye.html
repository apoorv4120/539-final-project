Skye

Initiator

Abilities

Regrowth

C

Equip and use Regrowth to heal yourself and nearby allies. Hold the ability key to channel healing over time.

Trailblazer

Q

Equip and deploy a Tasmanian tiger trinket. Fire to send the trinket forward, controlling its movement. Re-activate to transform the trinket into a flash that blinds nearby enemies.

Guiding Light

E

Equip and deploy a hawk trinket. Fire to send the hawk forward, controlling its movement. Re-activate to transform the hawk into a flash that blinds enemies in its line of sight.

Seekers

X

Equip a Seeker trinket. Fire to send out three Seekers to track down the three closest enemies. If a Seeker reaches its target, it nearsights them.
