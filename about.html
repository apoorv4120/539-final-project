About Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. The game was first announced with the codename Project A in October 2019 and was released on June 2, 2020. Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter where each player plays as an "agent" with unique abilities. The game is set on a near-future Earth and features a cast of characters known as "agents", each with their own unique abilities. Valorant is a team-based game, and players must work together to complete objectives and defeat the opposing team.

Gameplay

Valorant is a tactical shooter game that requires players to work together to complete objectives and defeat the opposing team. The game is played in rounds, with each round lasting 100 seconds. Players must work together to complete objectives and defeat the opposing team. The game features a cast of characters known as "agents", each with their own unique abilities. Players must choose an agent at the start of each round and use their abilities to outsmart and outmaneuver the opposing team. The game is fast-paced and requires quick reflexes and strategic thinking to succeed.
