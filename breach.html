Breach

Initiator

Abilities

Aftershock

C

Equip and fire an explosive charge into the target location. After a short delay, the charge detonates, dealing heavy damage to enemies within its radius.

Flashpoint

Q

Equip and fire a flash grenade. The grenade detonates after a brief delay, blinding all players in its line of sight.

Fault Line

E

Equip and fire a seismic blast forward. The blast deals damage to all players it passes through and causes a concussive effect, impairing the vision and audio of affected enemies.

Rolling Thunder

X

Equip and fire a massive seismic charge forward. The charge moves through walls and floors, and upon impact, it dazes and knocks up all players in its radius.
